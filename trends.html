Highlighting the Top Diamond Jewelry Trends for 2023 – By Rare Carat

lifestyle

You can show off your style with diamond jewelry, from solitaire earrings to diamond necklaces with matching bracelets and rings.

Diamonds come in different shapes and sizes, so there is something for everyone. In this article, let’s discuss the latest trends in diamond jewelry that can be eye-catching in 2023.

That being said, it’s now time for us to take a look at some of the hottest diamond jewelry trends:

 

1. Aurora Borealis Has Become Popular as a Diamond Design Element

Aurora Borealis is a rare and beautiful phenomenon caused by colliding charged particles with atoms in the atmosphere. It can be seen from Earth as it occurs at high latitudes in the northern hemisphere.

The auroras are mostly visible at night and appear green, red, or purple, depending on their origin. The most common color for an aurora is green because it occurs when excited oxygen atoms absorb energy from electrons. However, brightness varies as they can appear brighter than any other natural light emission.

While buying this trending design element, remember to choose a reputed diamond retailer. They offer an extensive selection of certified diamonds that the Gemological Institute of America and the American Gem Society have graded.

 

2. Floral Shapes and Designs Work Well with Diamond Jewelry

Floral shapes and designs work well with diamond jewelry. Floral shapes are a good choice for diamond jewelry. The flower symbolizes love, beauty, and joy, and it’s no wonder many women choose this design as their favorite shape for engagement rings or wedding bands.

Floral shapes can be a great way to show off your unique personality through the ring you wear daily. It features so much detail that will remind you of something special in your life every time you see it. You may consider Rare Carat as a platform to buy this ideal lifetime gift.

Choosing a floral shape is a great way to make your ring stand out. Diamonds are often associated with romance because they’re such beautiful stones, so it makes sense that you’d want to use this symbol of love in your engagement ring.

 

3. Rose Gold is Replacing White Gold When It Comes to Diamond Jewelry

Rose gold is an alternative to white gold because of its durability. Rose gold combines copper and gold, which makes it more durable than pure yellow or white metals.

The result is that rose gold can be worn for several years without showing signs of wear and tear, as some other types of jewelry might experience. In addition, it means that you won’t need to worry about replacing your diamond jewelry shortly as you might with colored metals, such as silver or platinum, provided you have purchased them from reliable retailers, like Rare Carat.

While rose gold is more expensive than traditional silver or yellow metals, such as 14k white gold and 18k yellow gold, it’s still cheaper than platinum. That makes it an excellent metal choice for your diamond jewelry.

 

4. Halo Settings are Becoming More Common for Diamonds

Halo settings are becoming more popular with the public. A halo is a row of small diamonds set in a circle around the center stone. It’s one of the best ways to show off your diamond and make it stand out.

Halo settings are well-known for wedding rings because they’re beautiful and timeless, but they also have another advantage. They can be less expensive than traditional solitaires. Thus, it makes them ideal for people who want something stunning without breaking their budget.

The same goes for engagement rings. The beauty of a ring like this is hard to beat. It is especially true if you want something traditional to go with any outfit or occasion but still fun enough to make an impression on anyone who sees it on your finger. According to Credit Donkey, American men spend approximately $5,225 on the engagement ring.

Anniversary rings work well here, too. The smaller stones surrounding each other create just enough sparkle while remaining elegant enough not to ruin anyone else’s view.

 

5. Pavé Settings are the New Rage for Diamond Earrings, Necklaces, and Rings

While diamond earrings, necklaces, and rings with pave settings are not new to the jewelry industry, they are becoming increasingly popular. This trend is driven by the fact that pave settings allow you to showcase multiple diamonds in one piece of jewelry.

A pave setting consists of a series of diamonds set into the metal of an item like a ring or pendant. The diamonds are placed together like paving stones on a sidewalk, giving them a sturdy and permanent appearance. Even after the cruelty of 2020, the global diamond jewelry market increased by 27% in 2021 compared to 2020, as per Statista.

While choosing from the options for your diamond jewelry pieces, 1-carat diamonds best suit earrings because they can be made more delicate than larger stones without losing their sparkle or value. For smaller stone sizes, such as half-carats or quarter-carats, it’s necessary to consider what type of metal you want before making your final decision.

 

6. Lab Grown Diamonds May Outsell Mined Diamonds by 2023

When choosing a set of diamond jewelry, it’s necessary to remember that all diamonds are not natural, and some of them are produced in laboratories. But what is a lab-created diamond?

Lab-grown diamonds or lab-created diamonds are created in a laboratory. They are also called synthetic diamonds, cultured diamonds, and imitation diamonds.

These stones are made of the same elements as mined and natural diamonds, but they’re created in a controlled environment instead of the deep underground by nature. Lab-grown diamonds have been around for decades, but almost all were an industrial grade or lower quality “bort.”

The technology for creating lab-grown diamonds has improved significantly over the last few years. As a result, an entire industry is dedicated to creating high-quality colorless or near-colorless lab-grown gems with similar chemical characteristics as mined stones.

However, you should be able to differentiate between high and poor quality, as many frauds are reported in the business. One thing you can do to save yourself from these frauds is to approach Rare Carat because their diamonds, whether natural or lab-grown, are certified by an independent gemologist. That, in turn, closes the doors for fraud to happen.

They are offered at much lower prices per carat weight than mined ones because these stones don’t require operations like open pit mining sites. Many people nowadays buy lab-grown gemstones instead of mined ones because they feel it’s better overall for society. According to Forbes, 2020 was strong for lab-grown diamonds because shoppers bought bigger diamonds but for less money.

 

If You Have the Money, Buy Big Diamonds Now

Diamonds aren’t just for engagement rings anymore. They make great gifts for birthdays and anniversaries, too. Thus, if you want to show how much someone means to you, consider getting them one, no matter what occasion you celebrate.

Buying diamond jewelry doesn’t have to be a stressful experience, especially when you know what to look for in terms of quality and price. However, if you’re looking for a big diamond, it’s best to buy one before it becomes too expensive.
